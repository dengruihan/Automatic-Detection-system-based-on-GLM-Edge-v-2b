About Golden Apple Snail

They have short tails and four tentacles on their head.

There color are yellowish and have cross lines on it.

They like to eat aquatic plants, especially rice straw

They have parasites inside their body.

They breed pink color eggs.

The Harm of Golden Apple Snails

Golden Apple Snails can spread quickly:

“One Golden Apple Snail can breed 200-1000 eggs.”

In this situation, it is possible for Golden Apple Snails breeding their eggs in every water area they want.
